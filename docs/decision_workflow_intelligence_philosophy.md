# Decision Workflow Intelligence Philosophy

Decision Workflow Intelligence connects valuation evidence to durable human governance. It does not approve, execute, or optimize organizational action. It preserves the path from a valued decision to review, approval, implementation, monitoring, outcome review, and learning.

## Governing Principles

### A Recommendation Is Not A Decision

AutoQuant may produce a governed recommendation, but an authorized human or governance body decides. Recommendation evidence must remain distinct from approval evidence.

### A Decision Is Not Implementation

Approval records what was authorized. Implementation records what actually happened. The two may differ, and the difference is analytically important.

### Implementation Is Not Outcome

A decision may be implemented faithfully and still produce an unfavorable outcome. It may also be implemented poorly and receive an apparently favorable outcome. Decision quality, implementation quality, and realized outcome must remain separate.

### Every Review Is Version-Specific

Reviewers approve or object to a specific decision context, alternative set, evidence package, valuation, recommendation, authority state, and evidence cutoff. Material edits should stale review or approval proportionally.

### Conditions Matter

Conditions attached to review or approval are not comments. They are first-class obligations with owners, due dates, evidence, status, breach severity, and resolution.

### Silence Is Not Approval

Missing review, expired approval, unresolved conditions, incomplete authority, and stale evidence are not permission.

### Governance Should Be Proportional

The workflow exists to preserve consequential reasoning and accountability, not to create bureaucracy. Required stages should scale with risk, magnitude, reversibility, scope, uncertainty, and authority.

### Realized Outcomes Complete The Decision

A decision remains open until implementation is known, expected outcomes have had time to mature, results are reviewed, and learning is preserved with clear scope limits.

## Scope Boundaries

This framework does not implement identity providers, electronic signatures, legal approval infrastructure, ERP integration, portfolio optimization, automated approval, or autonomous execution. It supplies deterministic contracts and evidence artifacts that higher-level systems can use.
